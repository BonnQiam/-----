Optimization & Trade-off¶

警告

Circuit design is a tradeoff of timing, power and area

Main Optimization Trade-Off s：

  • Timing optimization —— Goal: small delays，即通过时序优化以修复时序违例

  • Power optimization —— Goal: low power consumption

  • Area optimization —— Goal: small area
